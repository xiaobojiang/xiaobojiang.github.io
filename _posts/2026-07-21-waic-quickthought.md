### Robotics + AI 
In WAIC 2026, compared to 2025, my personal observation is that robotics takes a huge area of exhibition.

However, for robotics company the show is more about "muscle", how well I can control the arm and legs, and how I can use data capturing device to mimic human behavior.

When talked about robot think and act like human on general work, there is still a gap here. 

What showed here is on specific task: pack clothes, sorting packages

There are few claimed that they have trained brain model for generic thinking (LLM is not sufficient) on 3D world. Major demo is still remote control by human.

It is like early phase of EV cars. 

There are still few years to go, need to resolve computation capacity, power consumption and model breakthrough.

Long trip, challenges and opportunites ahead.
