The Year of the Horse is one of the 12 animals in the Chinese zodiac. In Chinese culture, the Horse symbolizes energy, freedom, strength, and passion. People born in the Year of the Horse are often seen as confident, hardworking, social, and adventurous. They are known for their lively spirit and strong desire for independence.

The Horse year comes every 12 years (for example: 1990, 2002, 2014, 2026). Each Horse year is also linked to one of the five elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water), which slightly influences the personality traits of that year.

Overall, the Horse represents movement, determination, and a drive to move forward in life. 🐎
