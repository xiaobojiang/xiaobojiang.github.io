What is disposable mail:

it is a mail service that you can get a short lived email address, and in the session, you can receive mails. 

Why we may need this:

I summarized two of mine frequently-used scenarios of disposable mail:

1, test purpose, verify application’s account creation and management feature

2, register an account in web, and avoid spams or advertisement to my mail box.

Many website provides those disposable mail service.

One of my recommendation:

https://10minutemail.com/

UI is simple:

1, you temporary email address, and inbox 

2, one count-down widget which tells how long the account is valid.

3, a button to get 10 more minutes

Page can be refreshed, the mail box will be kept if we do this.

Use this mail if applicable, and have fun.

Other disposable mail service used, features are similar:
