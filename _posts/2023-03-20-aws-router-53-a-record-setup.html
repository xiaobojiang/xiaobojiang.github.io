How to setup a A record, point desired subdomain to target ESK application load balancer url?

You may ask, why not cname.

CNAME to was alb’s DNS address, also works, but if you this, when you do nslookup, it will leak many details like your alb’s address, which is not nice.

The recommended way is to create a A record. 

Record type is A - Routers traffic to an IPv4 address and some AWS resources

Then enable “Alias”

And “Alias to Application and classic load balancer"

Last step, select the region and alb url in the dropdown.

And save.

Check the result with nslookup:

Nice! It has three records which are corresponding to 3 load balancer in three zones

That's it, keep in tune.
