This can be helpful if you want to host your service in China mainland.

Recently, I want to move my iotcolon.com from vultr server to Tencent cloud. I have purchased a really price-appealing 2Core2G server in Tencent cloud.

In order to host the service, I need to point the domain name to this new server.

My domain was purchased on Godaddy back to 2021, there is regulation which requires domain service provider shall be local and shall be registered with my identity.

This post will talk about my experience on this domain transfer.

--

First point, is that you need to find a domain service provider locally.

It is easy, Tencent cloud has this service, just use it.

--

Second, register your identity for domain usage

Create your information template, well, fill all the information, identity id, face recognition, email.

After all these, submit, you shall get your template approved within one hour.

--

Third, get the transfer code the domain from previous provider

For example, in Godaddy, go to your domain, click, "transfer domain from Godady”

Website will need your double confirmation, click “continue”.

In next page, fill the email address, you will get a code for your domain, copy the code to next step:

Finally, start transferring

Type the domain name and transfer code in following input.

It will check and go to next page, here it will ask you to renew additional one year. 

Choose the information template you had made in step 2.

Pay the money to renew 1 year, go to next step.

It is almost complete, you will get notification, it will take 3-7 working days to get final approval.

Once it is done, you can use it to apply for filing record for your cloud service.

Well, I will not detailed it here, follow the guide, and fill your information about website.

Tencent support team will do check and give you comments for improvement.

Optimize based on the comment, submit and wait for approval.
