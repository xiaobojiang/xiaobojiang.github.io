Same character, new background, new action — that’s the promise behind FLUX.1 Kontext, a new family of text-to-image models from Germany’s Black Forest Labs.

🆕 What’s New:

FLUX.1 Kontext (available in max, pro, and upcoming open-weight dev versions) specializes in character consistency and controlled image editing. Feed in a text prompt and reference image, and get back a coherent visual transformation where the identity of the character remains intact, even across different scenes, styles, and actions.

💡 Key Features:

  • Input: Text + Image → Output: Edited Image

  • Character consistency: Maintains key visual traits like face, clothing, and posture across generations

  • Local and global edits: Change small details (like facial expression) or whole scenes (like shifting the setting from forest to city)

  • Architecture: CNN encoder-decoder + Transformer (12B params in dev model)

💸 Pricing & Access:

  • Available via FLUX Playground and partners

  • FLUX.1 max: $0.08/image, FLUX.1 pro: $0.04/image (via Fal)

📊 Performance:

  • Outperforms OpenAI’s GPT Image 1 and Google Gemini 2.0 Flash in image editing and character preservation

  • Evaluated on a proprietary benchmark using 1,000+ crowd-sourced prompt/image pairs

  • FLUX.1 Kontext dev performs strongly, just behind FLUX pro/max and GPT Image 1 high-quality mode

🔍 Why It Matters:

Consistency is crucial for storytelling and branding — especially when reusing the same character across different scenes or campaigns. Earlier models often struggled with subtle shifts: a mole disappearing, hairstyle changing, or clothing morphing between shots. FLUX.1 tackles this by anchoring each generation to a reference image and prompt, ensuring a stable identity while still allowing creative control over setting and action.

🧪 What’s Next:

Black Forest Labs plans to release the FLUX.1 Kontext dev weights and its proprietary evaluation benchmark — a big step toward standardized testing in the generative image space.

✨ Whether you’re building comics, marketing visuals, or dynamic avatars — FLUX.1 Kontext brings precision and personality to your creative workflow.
