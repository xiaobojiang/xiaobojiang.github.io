Agent ascendant - promting skills, and multiple frameworks rises

Price tumble - price goes all the way down, GPT 4 2023 $10/30 per million token, in 2024 may GPT4o is $5/15, in August GPT4o  goes to %2.5/10. not to mention gpt4o-mini ($0.15/$0.6). thanks to competition and opensource models.

Generative video takes off - remarkable speed in models of text to video, even image to video and video to video. sora (openai), runway(runway), firefly (adobe, integrated into Adobe premiere pro), Movie Gen(meta, available to Instagram, with soundtrack and consistant face) and many powerful models in China

Smaller is beautiful - some models are small enough to run on mobile. Smaller models are becoming more capable due to tech like knowledge distillation (larger teacher model is used to train a smaller student model to match its output). Some techs like distillation, pruning, quantization. Top AI companies promoted family of model size, small, medium and large. Model families such as Microsoft Phi-3 (in versions of roughly 3.8 billion, 7 billion, and 14 billion parameters), Google Gemma 2 (2 billion, 9 billion, and 27 billion), and Hugging Face SmolLM (135 million, 360 million, and 1.7 billion).

Alternative to Acquisitions - big AI companies found creative ways to gain cutting-edge technology and talent without buying startups: hiring top executives and securing access to technology. But regulation is still monitoring this activity.
