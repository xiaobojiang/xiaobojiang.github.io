I have a very nice holiday in Qiandao Lake:
• Went rafting with the family
• Hit up a water park for some rides and slides
• Took jet skis out on the water — super fun
• Spent a good while practicing swimming too

Take a tour visiting few islands, from top, few islands builds an Chinese character "公"

Hotel is super nice, with wonderful view both in day and night!

Fully recommend!
