Share few nice photo shots of mine during holiday season.

1, Beatiful flower in BinJiang Forest Park in this autumn, we rent a bike and ride around. I always love this park, it is very beautiful, you can do camping, have picnics and fly kites. Best part, it is free of tickets.

2, My 18 years old mountain bike buddy, I bought it on 2007. Together we went a lot of places in Shanghai and beyond. I ride to Cao Wang Dian Temple in this Christmas.

3, Handcrafted baskets with bamboo slices, very common tooling in 90s. Still can be found now in Luodian Town.

4, Colorful metasequoia in late autunmn. Clustered trees in the water, like a island. Shot in Shanghu Park.

5, A glimpse of Bond in Shanghai, in it you can see the Huangpu River, and skylines of Pudong district.

Also you can see the Shanghai Tower, Shanghai World Financial center, and Oriental Pearl Tower (Toppest building, from right to left)

6, Music Gate in north bond. There are also three stainless steel sculptures on the grass (looks similar to me of the "Cloud Gate" in Chicago).

7, The "lady" statue, if you walk from WaiBaidu Bridge to the bond, you will notice this status in the park on your righthand. Wearing in grace dresses and nice hair-cuts, the lady is staring at Oriental Pear Tower.

What were the thoughts of her? Curious.

8, A memorial street lamp and notebook in Nanjing Dong Road. Mentioning that in 1882, the first commercial electricity generating company - Shanghai Electric went into operation. The very first electricity powered lamp stands right here.

hope you like this, and let's go for 2025.
