To chat with a PDF using Bing, you can use the Discover sidebar in the Microsoft Edge Dev browser. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Open up a PDF in your browser (it doesn’t even have to be online, it can be a local file).

Drag and drop the pdf in the bing chat window

Step 2: Click the Bing icon in the top right corner of the Edge Dev browser to open the Discover sidebar.

Step 3: Ask questions! Type “summarize this PDF” and Bing Chat will summarize the document for you.

    Wow, it works really nice, and you can ask any questions inside.

    It can really work as professional assistant to help read long and technical pdf.

    Good news for students and researchers!
